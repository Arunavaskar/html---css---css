Choosing for your site

A picture can say a thousand words and great images help
make the difference between a average looking site and a more engaging one!

Images can be used to set the tone for a site. There are a lots of companies that sell
stock images. These are images you pay to use.
You will have to always remember the fact that images online are always copyrighted and
using them without proper owner consent or any permit can lead to legal issues with the owner
of that image.

Things to remember:

Images should...

  1. be relevant
  2. convey information
  3. be instantly recognizable
  4. fit the color palette
